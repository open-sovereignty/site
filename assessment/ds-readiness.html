Sovereignty Readiness Assessment

A structured 21-question assessment across 7 sovereignty domains, weighted by your industry profile, that produces a maturity score and targeted improvement roadmap.

About this assessment

  • 7 domains — Data, Technical, Operational, Assurance, Open Source, Executive Oversight, and Managed Services
  • 21 questions — 3 per domain, each with contextual guidance
  • 3 answer options — Yes, No, or Don't Know (DK answers surface knowledge gaps)
  • Industry profiles — domain weights adapt to your sector's regulatory context
  • Maturity output — scored 0–100% against a 5-level maturity model with a tailored action plan

This assessment is for self-evaluation and educational purposes. It is not a formal certification or regulatory compliance tool. Results are calculated client-side and are not stored or transmitted.
